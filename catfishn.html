CatFishn

A user friendly video game!

Tech Stack: Java, JavaFX and FXGL Libraries

How to play

CatFishn is a simple fishing game where the player controls a cat trying to catch fish. The player uses the space bar to launch the fishing hook around the screen. The goal is to catch the Golden Fish.

To catch the Golden Fish, the player must use the Golden fishing rod which can be purchased at the in-game store. The Golden fishing rod costs 500 points, which can be earned by catching regular fish.
